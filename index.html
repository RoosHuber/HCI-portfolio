In this course I will learn to research and use Human Computer Interaction Technologies to come up with interactive concepts. In this course, not the user, but the technology is the starting point. I will research new interactive technologies, such as Playful Interfaces, Augmented and Virtual Reality, Wearables and Artificial Creatures. These technologies play a major role in the development of physical computing, virtual worlds, intelligent machines and other innovative applications. Together we will explore the exciting new possibilities of Human Computer Interaction Technologies.
